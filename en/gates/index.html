Gates – entrance systems and fittings

At the Metalzbyt Fencing Centre in Olsztyn we supply gates and pedestrian wickets as a complete addition to wire mesh and panel fencing. We help you choose the right solution together with the required fittings.

Available options include swing and sliding gates, with the possibility of installing a self-closing hinge. Typical sizes: widths from 3 m to 8 m and heights from 1 m to 2 m. Custom sizes can also be arranged to match individual requirements.
