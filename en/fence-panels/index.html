Fence panels – modern panel fencing

At the Metalzbyt Fencing Centre in Olsztyn you will find fence panels and systems that combine a clean look with long-term durability. We can help you choose the right set of components for your property.

Looking for other categories? See also: Wire mesh fencing, Gates and Steel gratings.

What you will find in our range

  • 2D fence panels – rigid panel fencing options in 6/5/6 and 8/6/8 wire configuration.
  • 3D fence panels – popular profiled panels in 4 mm and 5 mm wire thickness.
  • Panel heights: from 1 m to 2 m (matched to your property and privacy requirements).
  • Standard panel length: 2.5 m – easy planning by fence line length.
  • Posts, fixings and fittings – selected to match the chosen system and installation method.
  • Specialist advice – help choosing the correct panel type and a complete set for installation.
