Fencing Centre – Metalzbyt Olsztyn

The right fence combines durability, good looks and security. At the Metalzbyt Fencing Centre in Olsztyn, we help customers choose a system suited to the installation conditions and the finish they want – from mesh fencing, through panels, to gates.

We supply proven solutions for private properties and businesses. We match system elements (posts, clamps, connectors and fittings) so that installation is straightforward and the final result looks consistent. You can count on practical advice and an honest approach – based on durability, budget and lead time.

If you are planning fencing for a home, plot, business or development – contact us. We will help you choose the right system, calculate what you need and prepare a quotation.

We focus on quality, reliability and service you will want to come back to. Choose a solution that will perform for years.

We offer delivery of purchased fencing directly to your chosen location.

Wire mesh fencing

We help you assemble a complete wire mesh fencing system:

  • We manufacture galvanized (zinc-coated) wire mesh fencing in heights from 1 m to 2 m.
  • Standard roll length: 10 metres.
  • Available mesh types: chain link, welded mesh and woven mesh.
  • Posts and mounting components matched to the selected fencing system.
  • Accessories and fittings configured to fence length and installation conditions.
  • Expert advice for private and commercial applications.

Steel grating platforms – industrial and technical applications

At Metalzbyt Fencing Centre in Olsztyn, we supply steel grating platforms designed for a wide range of industrial and technical uses. They are commonly installed as pedestrian walkways and load-bearing routes for forklifts, pallet trucks and service vehicles.

  • Used in industrial facilities, transport infrastructure and technical sites.
  • Standard sizes from 300 × 1000 mm up to 1500 × 1000 mm.
  • Custom fabrication according to individual project specifications.
  • Welded or press-locked gratings – galvanized or stainless steel.
  • Complete fixing systems: clamps, connectors and mounting accessories.
