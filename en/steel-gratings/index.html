Steel gratings – industrial and technical use

At the Metalzbyt Fencing Centre in Olsztyn we supply steel gratings for industrial and technical applications. They are commonly used for pedestrian walkways and load-bearing routes for forklifts, pallet trucks and service vehicles.

We offer standard sizes as well as custom fabrication to match project requirements. You can also source complete fixing systems: clamps, connectors and mounting accessories.

Looking for other categories? See also: Wire mesh fencing, Panel fencing and Gates.

What we supply

  • Applications: industrial facilities, transport hubs, technical infrastructure and private projects.
  • Standard sizes: from 300 × 1000 mm up to 1500 × 1000 mm.
  • Custom fabrication according to project specifications.
  • Welded or press-locked gratings – galvanised or stainless steel.
  • Fixings and accessories: clamps, connectors and mounting components.
